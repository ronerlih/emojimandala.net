About Apple Emojis

Apple emojis are used on iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch and Apple TV. These are included as part of the Apple Color Emoji font.

WhatsApp uses Apple emoji images on all platforms, including Android. Custom stickers available in the iOS 10 Messages app supported for all iMessage users. Apple also has animated emoji stickers on Apple Watch.

New Emojis

iOS 10.2 was released on December 12, 2016. This release added support for Unicode 9 emojis, as well as new professions and a graphical redesign. Learn more.
